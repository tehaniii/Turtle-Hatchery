Turtle Hatchery


Making the world better for us and the small creatures living with us by giving back together.

Sri Lanka has a diversified sea turtle population, with five of the world's seven sea turtle species present in its seas. These turtles are a vital element of Sri Lanka's marine environment, helping to keep the ecosystem healthy and balanced. They help to manage the population of their victims, such as jellyfish and sponges, by servine as an indicator of the health of the marine environment.

Our Mission

Who we are

Our hatchery was established with the intention of boosting sea turtle numbers and provide a secure environment for hatchlings to develop and flourish. We are devoted to the preservation and protection of sea turtles at our turtle hatchery. We are a group of committed individuals working together to change the conservation landscape by spreading knowledge and taking action.


We hope to inspire the next generation of conservationists and increase public awareness about the dangers affecting sea turtles through our educational initiatives. Our turtle hatchery is dedicated to advancing conservation efforts and building a brighter future for sea turtles and their natural habitats.

What we do

By the implementation of several conservation projects, including as nest monitoring, egg protection, and hatchling release into the ocean, we tirelessly seek to preserve these wonderful sea turtles. With our outreach and education programs, we also hope to spread the word about the value of sea turtle conservation and inspire others to join us in our efforts.

Our hatchery was founded with the goal of protecting and conserving sea turtles and their habitats, and we have been working towards this mission ever since.We also believe that awareness and education are crucial to the long-term sustainability of sea turtle conservation, which is why we are committed to informing people about the issue and encouraging them to take action.

Why we do it

We are committed to sea turtle conservation at our turtle hatchery and want to save these incredible creatures for future generations. We are dedicated to doing our share to protect the survival of sea turtles because we understand the crucial role they play in preserving the balance and health of marine ecosystems.


We are working to boost sea turtle populations and bring marine ecosystems back into balance by safeguarding sea turtle nests and releasing hatchlings into the ocean.We operate as we do because we are committed to doing all in our power to ensure the survival of sea turtles and believe that every individual is capable of helping in the struggle to protect sea turtles and their ecosystems.
